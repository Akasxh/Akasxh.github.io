Why I Deactivated My Social Accounts

Apr 20, 2025 2 min read
Digital Wellbeing

For years I relied on messaging apps and social media to stay connected. Gradually I realised they were distracting me and affecting my well‑being.

Leaving these platforms gave me more time and clarity. This piece recounts my conversation with an AI assistant about the decision to log out and embrace more meaningful communication.
